Use Case 25: Improving Situational Awareness

Situational: Machine compromised

Structural: Fully patched, locked down endpoint,

due to malware installed through client-side attack

2FA enabled

Endpoint acting funny

Environmental: User of endpoint failed last phishing simulation test

Environmental: Training and awareness not complete

Contextual: User clicked on a spear phishing email
