Dejia Wang is the founder of PAYEGIS and has been the Chairman and CEO since 2011. Dejia founded PAYEGIS with the singular vision of empowering consumers and consumer-facing businesses to win the war on mobile, account and transaction fraud. Mr. Wang received PhD in Mathematics from University of Wisconsin-Madison, MS in Computability Theory from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and BSs in Probability and Statistics, Economical Management from University of Science and Technology of China.
