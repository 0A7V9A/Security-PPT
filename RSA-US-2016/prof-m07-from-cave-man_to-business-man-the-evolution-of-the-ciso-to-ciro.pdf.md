Speaking to the Board of Directors

#RSAC
Executive Management / Board ­ NACD
Guidance from the National Association of Corporate Directors (NACD)

PRINCIPLE 1: Cyber security is an enterprise risk management issue, not just an IT issue

PRINCIPLE 2: Understand legal implications of cyber risks

PRINCIPLE 3: Have regular updates and access to cyber security experts

PRINCIPLE 4: Establish cyber-risk management framework with adequate staffing and budget

PRINCIPLE 5: Discuss which risks to avoid, accept, mitigate or transfer through cyber insurance

Guidance includes specific questions about program maturity, breach notification, situational awareness, strategy and incident response

Source: nacdonline.org/cyber
