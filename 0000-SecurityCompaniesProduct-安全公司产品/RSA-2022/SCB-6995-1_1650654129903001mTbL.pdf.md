Description
The SCB-6995A is a desktop networking system. It supports Intel Atom® C3558R/C3758R (Denverton Refresh) Processor with QAT (encryption & decryption accelerator) for secure internet connection. The SCB-6995A support 6x RJ45 GbE and 2x 10G SFP+ ports. The SCB-6995A has 1x mPCIe and 1x M.2 slot for future expansion, e.g. WiFi/LTE/5G module. The SCB-6995A can connect with SATA/mSATA/eMMC storage. Customers can select suitable solution to meet their application.
