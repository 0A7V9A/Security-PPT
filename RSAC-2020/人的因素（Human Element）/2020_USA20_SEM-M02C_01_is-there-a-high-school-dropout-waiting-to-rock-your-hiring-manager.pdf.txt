Alan Turing: The man who cracked the `unbreakable' German Enigma Code in World War II.
It would have been impossible then for Turing to have had a formal diagnosis
Yet, today's experts in the field who have studied his life all reach the same conclusion: Alan Turing had Asperger's syndrome. He had autism.

An Official Definition of what a Disability is
A person with a disability has at least one of the following conditions Is deaf or has serious difficulty hearing; is blind or has serious difficulty seeing even when wearing glasses; has serious difficulty concentrating, remembering, or making decisions because of a physical, mental, or emotional condition; has serious difficulty walking or climbing stairs; has difficulty dressing or bathing; or has difficulty doing errands alone such as visiting the doctor's office or shopping because of a physical, mental, or emotional condition.
