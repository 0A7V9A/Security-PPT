ABOUT WCAPS
Women of Color Advancing Peace, Security, & Conflict Transformation's (WCAPS), Cybersecurity and Emerging Technology working group focuses on the impacts of emerging technology and cybersecurity on communities of color with an emphasis on women of color. This working group identifies, amplifies, & augments work being done to explore the effects of emerging technology and cybersecurity concerns on communities of color; promote the engagement of communities of color in the national and international discourse on relevant issues; and explore equipping communities of color to enter the workforce of the future. https://www.wcaps.org
