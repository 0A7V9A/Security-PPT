What Did We Learn?
The growing cybersecurity workforce gap of 4.07 million globally requires a 145% increase on the current 2.8 million in the field today For those who continually look for training and development opportunities, it's a great profession to move into, with good salaries and high job satisfaction Solutions include attracting new talent by selling them on career development opportunities, designing realistic hiring criteria that doesn't overlook those with non-traditional backgrounds, considering contractors, consultants or MSSPs, and cross-training motivated internal staff from other departments (IT, legal, communications, accounting, risk management, etc.)
