Apply What You Have Learned Today

Next week you should:
­ Check your roadmap to try and predict this year's sales and fraud spikes ­ Proactively sample and review fraud cases from sales spikes. Are they phishy?

In the first three months following this presentation you should:
­ Prepare a "spike-control" plan, including model training for high velocity ­ Consider raising end-user awareness to phishing schemes in your industry

Within six months you should:
­ Execute your plan and test it on your next seasonal/occasional spike ­ Monitor and expect lower fraud rates during your next sales spike
